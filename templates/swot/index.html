Company Overview

RAZCO Foods Ltd is one of Kenya's leading manufacturers and distributors of chilled dairy products, fruit juices, beverages and value-added food products. The company serves retail, wholesale, institutional and modern trade customers across the country through an extensive distribution network.

Along the Coast Region, the company continues to expand market penetration through distributor partnerships, direct key account servicing, structured route-to-market execution and continuous visibility initiatives that strengthen brand presence.

The Coast Growth Management System supports management in monitoring execution, identifying growth opportunities and improving commercial performance through accurate field data.
