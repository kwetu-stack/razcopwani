# RAZCO PWANI™
# Reports Module

---

## Purpose

The Reports Module is the Executive Intelligence Engine of RAZCO PWANI.

It consolidates information from all modules and transforms operational data into management insights, strategic reviews and decision-support reports.

The module provides visibility into business performance, growth opportunities, execution quality and strategic risks.

---

# Business Objective

Answer the following questions:

- Are we achieving growth targets?
- Which territories are performing?
- Which channels are growing?
- Where are the opportunities?
- Where are the risks?
- What actions require management attention?
