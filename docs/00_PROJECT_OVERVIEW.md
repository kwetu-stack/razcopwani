# RAZCO PWANI™

## Project Name

RAZCO PWANI™

---

## Description

RAZCO PWANI is a Coast Region Growth Management System designed to support the achievement of business growth targets across all sales channels.

The system captures market intelligence, customer information, opportunities, competitor activities, route execution and strategic actions in one centralized platform.

RAZCO PWANI is not a commercial product.

It is the digital representation of Harun's 20+ years of Coast FMCG experience.

---

## Mission

Grow Coast Region sales by 25% through disciplined execution, customer intelligence, opportunity management and strategic decision-making.

---

## Vision

To create a data-driven operating system that helps manage and grow the Coast business using the Kwetu philosophy.
