# RAZCO PWANI™
# System Architecture

---

## Architecture Philosophy

RAZCO PWANI is designed as a management operating system rather than a traditional CRM.

The architecture follows the actual flow of FMCG business operations.

The system moves from strategy to execution and finally to reporting.

---

# Layer 1: Strategic Layer

Purpose:

Define direction and understand the business environment.

Modules:

- Dashboard
- SWOT

Questions Answered:

- What is happening?
- Where are we strong?
- Where are we weak?
- Where are opportunities?
- What threatens growth?

---

# Layer 2: Market Layer

Purpose:

Understand the market and customers.

Modules:

- Customers
- Products
- Channels

Questions Answered:

- Who buys?
- What do they buy?
- Through which channels?
- Which products drive growth?

---

# Layer 3: Execution Layer

Purpose:

Manage field execution.

Modules:

- Routes
- Visits

Questions Answered:

- Are we covering the market?
- Are visits being completed?
- What is happening in the field?

---

# Layer 4: Growth Layer

Purpose:

Identify and protect future revenue.

Modules:

- Opportunities
- Competitors
- Lost Accounts

Questions Answered:

- Where will growth come from?
- What are competitors doing?
- Which customers can be recovered?

---

# Layer 5: Management Layer

Purpose:

Drive action and communication.

Modules:

- Action Plans
- Reports

Questions Answered:

- What actions are required?
- What progress has been made?
- What should management know?

# Core Design Principles

## Simplicity

Simple screens.

Simple workflows.

Fast data entry.

---

## Mobile Friendly

System must work on:

- Laptop
- Tablet
- Mobile Phone

---

## Data Driven

Decisions should be based on captured information.

---

## Action Oriented

Every observation should be convertible into an action.

---

## Scalable

Future modules can be added without redesigning the system.
